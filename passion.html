My BodyBuilding Journey

I started bodybuilding 1 year ago, along with my friends. I always had a thing for bodybuilding but I never try to commit myself to it, so for the first time ever I decided that once I start, I would not stop. From there I'have been going to the gym 4-5 days, a week for 1 year until now. At the first 2-3 months, it was really tough to give total commitment, but once I am hooked to it, its like I could never stop. I made a lot of progress in that 1 year like for instance, my starting point I was weighing around 50kg and now im already 69kg. I also gain a lot of muscle mass which is the primary goal but this is only the begining and I cant wait until the dietting down phrase which is a fat loss process.
